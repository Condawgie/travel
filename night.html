You haven't seen the city of Light until you've experienced the city at night. Throngs pour into popular streets, filling the air with the melody of engaged conversation and clinking glasses. This is when locals let down their hair and begin laughing and dancing, flirting and talking. Parisians love to savor life together.

    Bar & Pubs

  • Pop In: Basement for live music, a piano and a young crowd.
  • Le Motel: Down to earth, relaxed feel. Its love of music is shown by the concert posters decorated on its stone walls.
  • La Perle: A low key cafe whose 70s style ironically attracts a very young crowd.
  • Bulls Brothers: A petite bar that broadcasts sports games. Located in the cool area of Saint Michel around many other bars.
  • Chupitos: A very cheap shot bar with a lot of energy and young people.

    Clubs

  • Queens: Located in the 8th, a very common club among young people. Plays pop remixes, hip-hop, and house music.
  • Heritage: A very popular club, although you have to dress to impress and reserve a table.
  • Duplex: A two story club; the top floor is a hip-hop area and below is a house music club.
  • Titty Twister: A very popular club that plays house and hip-hop music.
  • Cartel: This club is always poppin with more hip-hop music.
