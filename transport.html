Metro

The ever-expanding metro and RERtrain systems transport parisians just about anywhere they want to go. It is cheap and efficient. A one way ticket starts at $2 and a day pass is $14.

Bus

Just like the metro and RER, the bus has very many stops and will take you almost anywhere you need in Paris.

Velib

If you do not have far to go, many people will either walk or grab a velib which is a community bikes with many stations scattered around Paris.

Taxi

Taking a taxi is not very popular because of the extremely high prices of a taxi but they can be found everywhere.

Uber

More popular than taking a taxi, if you cannot walk taking an Uberpool is an easy way to get around.
